The Internet We Imagined vs. Reality

The Internet Today: Not as Free as We Imagined

When the internet first emerged, we envisioned a free, open, and limitless space where anyone could share ideas, connect, and innovate. However, today the internet is increasingly dominated by a few tech giants, surveillance systems, and algorithms that prioritize profits over privacy and freedom.

The promise of an open and accessible internet is fading as we see more corporate control, censorship, and data exploitation taking center stage. As users, we must reconsider the way we navigate the digital world.
