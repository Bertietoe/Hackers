Jaron Lanier's Talk on How the Internet Failed

In his influential talks, Jaron Lanier has critiqued how the internet has evolved. Instead of fostering creativity, connection, and openness, it has become a tool for data mining, surveillance, and manipulation. Lanier's view is that the internet’s original promise has been hijacked by powerful corporations, turning it into a platform for profit at the cost of personal freedom.

The Movie Hackers (1995)

The 1995 movie Hackers portrays a subculture of young hackers challenging authority and fighting against the corrupt powers that control the digital landscape. The film captures the spirit of rebellion and the original vision of the internet, offering a stark contrast to today’s corporate-dominated cyberspace.
